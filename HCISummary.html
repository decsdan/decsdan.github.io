CarlSwap

Created by:

Daniel Scheider, Lucky Beulla Muhoza, Owen Xu, and Kritika Pandit

Our project aims to help make the Item Exchange at the end of the year easier by streamlining the buying and selling of items throughout the school year, and by logging the items sold to students during new student week. Our goal is to make things easier on CCCE without impacting their affiliated nonprofits.

This prototype addresses user needs, as it has a way for students to buy and sell items throughout the year, making things easier on CCCE and ECO. This also includes a login, meaning ONLY carleton students will be utilizing this app/website. This login will also have admin accounts (CCCE members), who will have access to what gets bought/sold, as well as a way to document what gets bought and sold during NSW.
